Turret data

This page contains data for every turret in my game, TDG.
The game already provides information for them, but there just isn't enough space for extra stuffs to be added.
Some turrets mentioned here that are not in the game yet may be added within the upcoming updates.
For turrets with special upgrades & abilities, visit the Special upgrades page.

Freezing snowstorm
(ability)
 Chance for summoning a snowstorm.
A snowstorm can spawn snowflakes (20 of them) that target random locations. On impact, applies freezing effect for enemies within it's range (freezes slow enemies such as Strong and all bosses).
While a snowstorm is happening, all enemies receive double damage (in other words, all turrets deal double damage).		 Snowflake base damage: 5
All levels: +5% (20% total)

Meteor shower
(ability)
 Chance for summoning a meteor.
A meteor can target a random enemy location (even dead ones). On impact, burns all enemies and deal massive damage.
The damage for meteors increase by 1 every 25 waves.		 Meteor base damage: 7
Levels 1-3: +2% (+4% as of 1.8.1)
Max level: 4% (+8% as of 1.8.1)
