Enemy data

This page contains a ton of data for enemies in my game, TDG.
Some enemies mentioned here that are not in the game yet may be added within the upcoming updates.
If you have any questions, please ask me (I won't give you my contact informations tho).

Normal enemies

This enemy is displayed as a orange circle.
It has a slow moving speed, but has decent health.
Can show up in almost every wave as a default mob.

  • Move speed: 3
  • Turn speed: 6
  • Base health: 3
  • Cash drop amount: $1

Fast enemies

This enemy is displayed as a yellow circle.
Although it has a low health, it can move really fast, so be careful.
Can show up in almost every wave as a default mob.

  • Move speed: 5
  • Turn speed: 11
  • Base health: 2
  • Cash drop amount: $2

Strong enemies

This enemy is displayed as a red circle.
It moves slow, but has a ton of health.
Fast enemies can act as a "meat shield" for these guys.
Can show up in almost every wave as a default mob.

  • Move speed: 2
  • Turn speed: 5
  • Base health: 5
  • Cash drop amount: $4

Hypersonic
enemies

This enemy is displayed as a dark blue/blue circle.
Although it has a low health, it can move really fast, so be careful.
Only show up in later waves as a late-game enemy.

  • No base data available for this enemy, although they will
    drop the same amount of cash as Strong enemies.

Flame
enemies

This enemy is displayed as a dark orange circle with a flame emoji inside.
It has slow move speed but decent health, and is Fire-resistant.
Only show up in later waves as a late-game enemy.

  • Move speed: 3
  • Turn speed: 6
  • Base health: 4
  • Cash drop amount: $6

Freeze
enemies

This enemy is displayed as a light-blue circle with a snowflake emoji inside.
Though pretty slow, it can be dangerous as it is Snow-resistant.
Only show up in later waves as a late-game enemy.

  • Move speed: 2
  • Turn speed: 5
  • Base health: 5
  • Cash drop amount: $5

Lightning
enemies

This enemy is displayed as a light-yellow circle with a lightning emoji inside.
Not too fast, but not easy to kill either. These are Electric-resistant.
Only show up in later waves as a late-game enemy.

  • Move speed: 4
  • Turn speed: 8
  • Base health: 4
  • Cash drop amount: $6

Acid (poison)
enemies

This enemy is displayed as a green circle with a biohazard emoji inside.
It has slow move speed but decent health, and is Poison-resistant.
Only show up in later waves as a late-game enemy.

  • Move speed: 3
  • Turn speed: 6
  • Base health: 3
  • Cash drop amount: $6

Normal boss

This boss is displayed as a dark-blue circle, being slightly bigger than others.
They are slow but have a huge amount of health, and their health scales
propotionally to the current wave count.
Shows up every 10 waves, and can spawn anytime.

  • Move speed: 2
  • Turn speed: 4
  • Base health: 65 (wave 10)
  • Cash drop amount: $32 (wave 10)

Regen boss

This boss is displayed as a dark-red circle, being slightly bigger than others.
They are slow but have a huge amount of health, and their health scales
propotionally to the current wave count.
Only spawns from wave 15 and beyond, and can spawn anytime.
Does not spawn on boss waves.
Their spawn chance increases with each wave.

  • Move speed: 2
  • Turn speed: 4
  • Base health: 45 (wave 10)
  • Cash drop amount: $24 (wave 10)
