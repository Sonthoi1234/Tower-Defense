Special abilities

This page contains all the information about special abilities (for turrets).

Ability name Upgrade name How it works Ability stats
Meteor
(from Fire turret)
 Meteor shower
 A meteor can target a random enemy location on the map.
On impact, burns all enemies and deal massive damage.		 Base damage: 7 Increases by 1 every 25 waves
Ice cubes
(from Snow turret)
 Raining cubes
 Cubes can fall on a random location with different sizes.
On impact, deals damage (?) and stuns all enemies within it's range.		 Base damage: ???
Snowstorm
(from Snow turret)
 Freezing snowstorm
 A snowstorm can spawn snowflakes that target random locations.
While a snowstorm is happening, all enemies receive double damage.		 Number of snowflakes spawn: 20
Snowflake
(from Snow turret)
 Freezing snowstorm
 A snowflake can target any random location with different sizes.
On impact, applies freezing effect for enemies within it's range (stuns slow enemies such as Strong and all bosses).		 Base damage: 5
TNT
(from TNT turret)
 Default ability TNTs are placed on the front-most enemy.
They explode after, what, 2 seconds? (I never checked again lol).
The blast radius is small enough for the damage it does.		 Base damage: 9 Increases by 1 every 15 waves
Crater
(from TNT turret)
 Death crater
 The crater freezes enemies regardless if they are cooking steak on their head or not (that's a bad joke).
TNTs spawned on craters deal double damage.		 Spawn duration: 5sec
Cyclone
(from Cyclone turret)
 Default ability Cyclones travel in the opposite direction from the enemies.
Enemies touching the cyclone take constant damage.
On a snowstorm, cyclones move at half their speed but deal double damage and last longer.
That's why they are hard to use.		 Spawn duration: 5sec
